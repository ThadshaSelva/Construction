Who We Are

Built on Trust,
Driven by Excellence

Founded in 2009, NR Plus Construction & Engineering has grown from a small team of passionate builders into one of Colombo's most respected construction and engineering firms. We have delivered over 250 residential and commercial projects across the greater Colombo area.

Our philosophy is simple: every client deserves a home or office that reflects their unique vision. We combine the latest structural engineering techniques with beautiful architectural design to create spaces that stand the test of time.

From our head office in Kollupitiya, we serve clients across Colombo 01–15, Nugegoda, Maharagama, Rajagiriya, Battaramulla, Nawala, Dehiwala and beyond. We are proud to be a 100% Sri Lankan-owned company that employs local engineers, architects and tradespeople.
