Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does it take to build a house?

A 1-storey home typically takes 6–9 months. A 2-storey home 9–14 months, and a 3-storey luxury residence 14–20 months, depending on finishes and site conditions.

Do you help with building permits?

Yes. NR Plus handles the full CMC (Colombo Municipal Council) approval process including architectural drawings, structural plans and all permit applications.

What is the minimum budget to start?

Our projects start from LKR 8 million for a basic 1-storey home. We provide a detailed BOQ before any commitment so you can plan with confidence.

Can I visit a completed project?

Absolutely. We can arrange showroom visits and, with client permission, tours of recently completed homes. Contact us to organise a visit.
