Who We Are

Colombo's Trusted
Construction Partner

NR Plus Construction & Engineering is a premier building company based in Colombo, Sri Lanka. We specialise in designing and constructing custom residential homes — from compact single-storey villas to grand three-storey family residences — as well as modern commercial and office buildings.

Our team of experienced engineers and architects works hand-in-hand with every client to turn their vision into a home they'll cherish for generations.
